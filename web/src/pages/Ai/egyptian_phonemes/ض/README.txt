Recording Instructions for Letter: ض

1. File naming: ض_test1.mp3, ض_test2.mp3, etc.
2. Record in different ways:
   - Normal speed
   - Slow speed  
   - Fast speed
   - High pitch
   - Low pitch
   - With emotion
   - Whispering
   - Loud voice

3. Record at least 10 different versions
4. Use good quality microphone
5. Record in quiet environment

Example files to create:

ض_slow1.mp3
